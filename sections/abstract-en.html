Abstract

The Internet of Construction Process Ontology (ioc) is a top-level ontology for describing processes and process-data in the domain of construction. It is a ongoing work as part of the research project “Internet of Construction” lead by Prof. Dr. Sigrid Brell-Cokcan. The research is funded by the Federal Ministry of Education and Research of Germany within the indirective on a joint funding initiative in the field of innovation for production, services and labor of tomorrow (funding number: 02P17D081).
